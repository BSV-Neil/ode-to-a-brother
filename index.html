This site is dedicated to extolling the virtues of my brother, Mark. Sure, I could tell all sorts of "little brother" stories about him - like the time he spilled the whole pitcher of lemonade all over the kitchen floor! - but the reality is that he's the bestest brother on the planet. Read on to find out why.

Why we're glad you're here

  • Someone has to make stupid puns with me.
  • You know what it means to impersonate Steve Martin doing that walking-sucker-hand-thing in Roxanne.
  • I need someone to call whenever "Tainted Love" comes on.
  • I don't know anyone else who've ever raced an '86 Volvo stationwagon.
  • You get me.

Milestones

  • Sometime in the early 80s: you were born, and I became your big sister. Hooray!
  • Sometime in the late 80s: you and Garrett perfected the art of scooter-racing the Hot Wheels.
  • Sometime in the early 90s: I got to keep an eye on you during the summers. That basically meant I dragged you everywhere with me and my friends. You took it pretty well (I think).
  • Sometime in the early 2000s: you met this gal named Kristen. You went to an Indigo Girls concert. The rest is history.
  • Sometime around 2010: You could start legitimately receiving email addressed to "Dr. Lingwood." The doctor is in!
  • Sometime in the early 2010s: move where there are Waffle Houses. Move away from where there are Waffle Houses. Start transforming impressionable college-aged minds.
  • Sometime in the mid 2010s: help create not just one, but two little amazing people.
  • Sometime in the late 2010s: have a birthday, for which I made you my site. Thanks for being my brother! Keep it up!
